# Example: Establishing Agile Project Management and Cross-Functional Collaboration to Improve Product Delivery at Cisco Systems

## Context  
While working as an Engineering Manager at Cisco Systems, I managed a full-stack development team of 8–12 engineers focused on cloud and networking SaaS solutions. The team was facing challenges with project delays, unclear prioritization, and coordination issues across multiple geographies and business units, which impacted delivery timelines and customer satisfaction.

## Management Process Implemented
To address these challenges, I established a structured Agile project management process combined with enhanced cross-functional collaboration:

- **Agile Ceremonies:** Implemented regular Scrum ceremonies including sprint planning, daily stand-ups, backlog grooming, sprint demos, and retrospectives to improve transparency and team accountability.
- **Backlog Prioritization:** Worked closely with Product Owners, PMs, and Directors to prioritize the backlog based on customer impact, technical feasibility, and business goals.
- **Resource and Risk Planning:** Developed detailed project execution plans with resource allocation, risk assessment, and mitigation strategies to proactively manage potential blockers.
- **Cross-Functional Collaboration:** Facilitated communication between engineering, product management, customer support, and marketing teams to ensure alignment on requirements and timely resolution of escalations.
- **Mentorship and Talent Development:** Focused on mentoring engineers, conducting one-on-one sessions, and fostering a culture of continuous learning and performance improvement.

## Results  
- **On-Time Delivery:** Improved sprint delivery rate from approximately 65% to 92% within 6 months by enforcing Agile discipline and better backlog management.
- **Customer Escalation Resolution:** Reduced average customer issue resolution time by 30% through improved coordination between engineering and support teams.
- **Team Productivity:** Increased velocity by 25%, measured by story points completed per sprint, due to clearer priorities and reduced blockers.
- **Employee Engagement:** Achieved higher team satisfaction scores in internal surveys, reflecting improved morale and motivation.
- **Hiring and Team Growth:** Successfully hired and onboarded 5 new engineers, building a high-performance team aligned with strategic goals.

## Impact  
This management process not only enhanced the team’s ability to deliver high-quality cloud networking products on schedule but also improved customer satisfaction and operational efficiency. The structured Agile approach and strong cross-team collaboration created a scalable framework that supported rapid innovation and growth in a complex, multi-tenant SaaS environment.
